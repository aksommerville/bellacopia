2025-12-28
Minigames, sorted roughly by estimated effort to implement.
Remove finished games as you go.

## Easy
Fire. L/R fast to fill a bar and start the fire. (rubbing sticks)
Fission. Tap A until the atom splits.
Flapjack. A to flip your pancake. Aim to cook both sides evenly.
Flapping. Tap A.
Frog Eating. A to grab and eat a frog, when it hops near.
Gobbling. A to grab and eat, L to pull the tablecloth closer.
Distancing. Dpad to get as far from everyone as possible.
Dodging. Bullet hell.
Ceiling Touching. U to touch the ceiling, and that bit changes color. Standing on stilts, decoratively.
Cheese Cutting. A to drop your guillotine, when a flying cheese is under it.
Arm Wrestling. Tap A, hold your power meter high.
Balancing. Walk to the goal with a tomato on your head.
Body Slam. Jump and press Down at the peak, to fall fast and slam thru layers of ice.
Bouncing. Squirrels throw acorns from their trees, stand under to bounce it. Horizontally symmetric, both players bounce acorns from both sides.
Burrowing. Right to move, A to dig. Dig thru the dirt.
Cake Carrying. On a train that stops suddenly. L/R to balance.
Cheating. Three-card monte but you'll always lose. A to punch the guy, and win.
Clam Baking. Hold A to engage flamethrower, only when the clam is open.
Climbing. L/R and A/B, maintaining one "point of contact" at all times.
Conducting. U/D to control train's throttle. Cross a bridge as a pair of fish builds it.
Counting. Lots of moving things and you have to count one class of them.
Crossing. Frogger.
Diving. R/L to move, hold A to jump. Easier to move before releasing A.
Drawing. Steal from https://fkglr.itch.io/ikkoky , if he approves. Hold A until the other gunslinger twitches, then you have a very narrow window to release.
Extinguishing. Walk around and A to put out fires. Extinguisher takes a sec to charge up, and inhibits movement slightly when engaged.
Fanning. Fan the princess as she walks by, but you can't fan and walk at the same time.
Fencing. Tap A at optimal rhythm to add planks to a fence.
Harvesting. Walk Left and Right, A to pick a sprout a la Mario 2.
Hurdles. Track and Field. Tap A to run, and Up to jump.
Imitation. Simon Says. (like Sumo Horse but no timing, full dpad, and you're both following an NPC Simon).
Jeter. Rotate automatically, hold A to charge, then release to throw the ballerina. Pillows to the right and briars to the left.
Jewelry. Rings and necklaces fall from the sky. L/R to walk, then B/A to hold up a hand for rings, or neither to collect a necklace. And dodge the bird with a magnet.
Juggling. Standing on a broom, Left and Right to balance, and tap A at the right moments to continue juggling.
Jumprope.
Karate Chop. Tap A until time expires.
Levitation. Keep an indicator balanced.
Licking. Hold A when the giant moving ice cream cone is in range.
Lighting. Walk and jump to reach the candles, as an acolyte.
Mixing. L/R to pour Blue or Red into your blender. Match a reference Purple without spilling.
Newspaper. Throw newspapers across the street into mailboxes.
Pairing. Pick the matching sock. First to pick it gets a point.
Parachute. Deploy as late as you dare, last to land alive wins.
Parkour. Tap A at the right moment to wall-jump.
Petrifying. Put on a Medusa mask as the knight approaches, to catch him at the right moment for a handsome statue.
Photography. Tap once, as a bat flies past the moon.
Piano Throwing. Tap A to charge, then B to release.
Pilfering. Dig Dug style, dig up to steal carrots, fail if you break the surface.
Pitching. Circle the dpad then tap A, judged on consistency of timing.
Pouring. Once chance to hold A and release. Speeds up as it goes. Disqualify if any overflow.
Protecting. U/D to deflect incoming bombs, keep them away from the gaggle of chicks.
Pushing. Push a statue to the target cell. Sokoban-ish.
Ramming. Take turns with the Orca, bashing your head against a boat, whoever sinks it wins.
Rebounding. Pong. First miss loses.
Remembering. Shown 9 cards, then they flip and you have to pick a given one.
Rodeo. L/R to balance on the bull or hold A to brace.
Rollerskates. Jump and brake. Gets too fast if you don't brake.
Rowing. Circle the dpad with lopsided speed, slower in the water.
Sawing. L/R, and there's an ideal rhythm.
Scaring. A once to jump out of the bushes as the man walks by. Find the optimal position.
Shaking. U/D to shake your bottle, then the cork flies off when time expires. Highest cork wins.
Sharing. Dpad around the crowd, touch somebody to give them a berry.
Shovelling. Hold A to scoop dirt, then drop it in moving mine carts below.
Shuffling. Tap to commit. First pick a cut point, then merge phase. Ideal is exactly half on both.
Skipping. Tap A to skip a stone, tap at each hop and they get shorter with lower tolerance as it goes.
Slapping. One chance to press A, to slap a given card when it falls on the pile.
Slicing. Tap to stop the blade and cut the pie, aiming for half.
Sliding. Tap R to run, then A to jump onto the ice, L to backpedal. Track and Field ish.
Smashing. Stand still, A to jump. Squash melons as they roll at you.
Snorkel. Hold A to blow thru your snorkel at a ping-pong ball, try to maintain a middling elevation.
Snowball Fight. Once around the dpad, then A to throw it, then repeat.
Stacking. L/R to walk and catch falling pancakes on your two plates. Fall if too lopsided.
Staring. Dpad to keep your eyes on the foe's.
Stirring. Circle the dpad.
Strangling. Against the Root Devil. Fight to the death.
Sumo Horse. Metronome. One player dances with only Left and Right, then the other player must repeat it.
Surrounding. Snake.
Swearing. Press the button for a word bubble like "$#@!", last word wins.
Swinging. Hold L/R in phase, riding a swing.
Telekinesis. Tap A until the bottle of Old Janx Spirit tips. (ends when either reaches a target intensity)
Topping. Hold A to pour ketchup on a giant moving hotdog.
Traffic. Stand in one of four quadrants of an intersection to pass two of four lanes of traffic.
Umbrella. Hold A while raining, release while not.
Watching. Tiny Dot walks around a giant stovetop with four pots. Look at one to prevent it from boiling.
Watermelon. Hold A to charge and release to spit a seed, going for distance.

## Hard
CPR.
Whining. Get mama bear's attention by tapping U when she's looking and A when not.
Wining. Big pile of grapes that you jump on to squash.
Wrapping. Place the toy in the middle of the wrapping paper, and it wraps itself. Score is how close to center.
Bombing. Mini Super Bomberman?
Breaching. L/R to turn, tap A to accelerate. Try to hit the surface perpendicular and at maximum velocity. (jumping whale)
Broom Race. See `apothecary`.
Building. Mini platformer. Walk, jump, lift, and drop to reach your bell first.
Cartography. Pick a color for all the regions of a map, 4 choices, no going back.
Cinematography. Bat flies across the screen, you have a rectangle that can move and zoom. Keep it in frame as tight as possible.
Cutting. Auto-sweeping line, press A to commit angle and hold to continue cutting a straight line. Match the pattern.
Dance Off. Tiny rhythm game on the dpad.
Dissection. Dpad to place scalpel, then hold A to select a direction, then tap A to select length. Cut the arteries but don't touch the organs.
Floe Chart. Dizzying grid of ice floes with arrows to a neighbor. Pick an entry point.
Flipping. Bounce on a trampoline. Well-timed A to bounce higher. L/R to turn. First to flip and land feet-down wins.
Flying. One-button bird race a la Reddin Iggle or Flappy Bird.
Golf. Whack the ball in a closed room, it bounces off all the walls, try to land as far away as possible.
Home Run Derby. A at the right moment, ball lands in 1BH, 2BH, 3BH, or HR.
Hopscotch. Tap the indicated button as Dot lands on them. Maybe sync to the music?
Joust. Joust, the video game. You on a broom, and he on Pegasus.
Lawnmowing. Visit as much of a grid as possible without retracing any steps.
Math. 10-key input, answer simple arithmetic problems.
Mind Control. Alternate A/B to maintain control, and dpad to move your victim. Have him push the candy to you.
Plumbing. Rotate columns in a 4x4 Pipe Dream puzzle to complete a path.
Popping. Giant robot thing with a laser, shoot the cornfield to pop kernels.
Rat Race. Race to the cheese, in a maze. Constant forward velocity and relative L/R.
Robohorse. L/R control the horse's two legs. QWOP-ish.
Scuba Tuba. Rhythm game on the dpad while tapping A to kick, to stay in the middle of the water.
Skiing. L/R and brake, get between the flags.
Snaring. One button. Hear a snare riff, then repeat it, with a metronome.
Sonar. 1st-person. See nothing but a compass. L/R to rotate, A to screech and it shows you the distance. U to advance.
Sorting. Columns-ish, put falling things in like piles.
Sparing. Bowling, second throw only.
Steering. Drag race with only L/R, dodging critters etc.
Stenography. Tap all the buttons in order, following a sheet.
Tempting. L/R to keep an indicator balance and note on tune, sailor moves toward the more enchanting song.
Tic Tac Toe. The whole game.
Weaving. A weft thread moves horizontally on its own, U/D to alternate between warps.
Wrecking. Mini platformer full of fragile things and you have a giant hammer.
Calligraphy. Paint a reference figure.
Morse Code. Tap out a short message with A. Key visible on screen, but you can only win higher diffs by remembering.
Latin. Show a Linnean plant or animal name and three pictures. First to pick it right gets a point. Repeat a few times.
Chess. Canned scenario where you have one move to make mate. (opponent doesn't actually play)
